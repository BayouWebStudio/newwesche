The Discipline

Sacred Geometry
on Skin.

Large-scale sacred geometry, mandalas, and Platonic solids. Each piece is built over a full-day session — no rushing, no compromising. Over a decade of work that still feels like it's pointing at something real.

I specialize in the kind of geometry that has existed for thousands of years: Metatron's Cube, the Flower of Life, the Vesica Piscis, nested Platonic solids. Patterns with mathematical precision and something harder to name underneath them.

People fly in from across the country — and occasionally further — which still surprises me. I work out of Tattoo Temple Houston. I take it seriously.

Before You Book

What to Expect

◎

Full-Day Commitment

Every session is a full day. Large-scale sacred geometry demands it — and deserves it. Plan accordingly. No abbreviated sessions for large pieces.

⬡

Custom Designs Only

No flash, no templates, no copying existing work. Every design is built from scratch for the specific body, placement, and intent of the person wearing it.

△

Mathematically Precise

Sacred geometry done right is exacting work. The proportions are real, the relationships are real. It shows in the finished piece — and it shows when they're not.

✦

Large Scale Specialty

Full sleeves, back pieces, chest panels. Small pieces occasionally. The geometry wants room to breathe — small formats compromise what makes it meaningful.

◇

Houston, TX

Based at Tattoo Temple Houston. People travel — some from across the country, some from further. Worth noting when you're planning a session.
