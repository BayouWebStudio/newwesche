Common Questions

Before You Reach Out

Are books actually closed right now?

Yes. I periodically open a limited number of slots. Submit an inquiry to join the waitlist and I'll reach out when the next opening happens. Usually a few times per year.

Do you do small tattoos?

Occasionally. The geometry I specialize in — sacred geometry, Platonic solids, full mandalas — needs room to breathe. Small formats usually compromise the precision. I'll be honest about it during consultation.

How long is a typical session?

Full day. Large-scale sacred geometry requires it — and deserves it. Plan for 6–9 hours depending on complexity and what your body can handle in a sitting.

Do you travel to guest spots?

Occasionally. If you're interested in organizing a guest spot or have a studio in mind, include that in your inquiry. I consider it when it makes sense.

What's your rate?

I charge a day rate rather than an hourly rate. Rate depends on the complexity and scale of the piece. We'll discuss this after an initial consultation — not before. I don't quote via DM.

Will you copy someone else's tattoo design?

No. Every piece is custom and designed from scratch. If you send reference images, they inform the direction — they're not reproduced. I won't copy another artist's work.
